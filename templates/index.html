The Problem

Inefficient knowledge exchange

M&A tax advisors have poor access to existing in-house knowledge: Often emailing between teams to exchange documents.

Underutilization of professionals

Professionals spend hundreds of hours manually rewriting advice from scratch, and tax legislation changes often make past work redundant or prone to causing mistakes.

Profits are stalling

Profits across the Big 4 and other professional service firms are stagnant or falling.

The Solution

A targeted end-to-end workflow that increases the fluidity of in-house knowledge, while automating manual processes in M&A tax.

1. Knowledge bank

  • Securely stores embedded tax deliverables.
  • Controllable and accountable access across teams enhancing regulatory compliance.
  • AI-generated metadata to enhance findability.

2. RAG

  • Retrieves firm-wide tax insights and client-reviewed analysis instantly.
  • Prevents unnecessary exposure of non-relevant documents.
  • Enables access to files wider than a professional's network.

3. LLM

  • AI-powered drafting that leverages in-house knowledge found in RAG.
  • Latest frontier models, trained on current legislation and industry best practices, enable refinement of legacy cases.
